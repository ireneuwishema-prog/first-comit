Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I register?

Click the Register button on the home page, fill in your details, and wait for approval.

❓ Who can use this system?

Students who want to learn, Stagers who want to teach, and Managers who oversee the system.

❓ Is it free to use?

Yes, the basic system is free for all registered users.

❓ How do I book a session?

After logging in, go to the Booking section and fill out the booking form.
